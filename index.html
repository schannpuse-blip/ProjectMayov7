17. Dea Thea Badaguas

What did she do for you to think that she is a really great friend to you?

"I never really knew Cheska that well before, even though we studied in the same elementary school and even became classmates in Grade 9. Back then, she was someone I only knew through familiarity or as an acquaintance. But by chance, we became classmates again in Grade 10, and that’s when I truly got to know her. I was honestly surprised by how easygoing and comfortable she was to be around. She’s the type of person who genuinely expresses herself no matter who she’s talking to. But spending Grade 10 with Cheska slowly changed that. Through her personality, humor, and the way she freely showed who she was, I learned how to become more expressive and confident with myself too. No more constantly holding back. Even if I never really told her this directly, her presence gives people this light and comfortable feeling that naturally brings out laughter, confidence, and authenticity. And that’s one of the biggest reasons why she became such a great friend to me."
